## MARKET CONTEXT: WHY NOW?

### Manufacturing Facing Three Pressures

1. **Labor Shortage**  
   - Quality inspectors are increasingly hard to hire and retain
   - Manual inspection is slow and error-prone
   - Companies are desperate for automation

2. **Quality Compliance Tightening**  
   - Aerospace, medical devices, automotive require traceability
   - Regulators want proof of consistent quality
   - Customers want data, not just "we inspected it"

3. **AI Maturity Crossing Threshold**  
   - Computer vision models now 95%+ accurate
   - Cloud infrastructure commoditized and cheap
   - Customers expect AI-driven products from their suppliers

**Your Competitive Window:** 2-3 years before competitors catch up

## SEVEN REASONS THIS PLATFORM WINS THE MARKET

### 1. **Vertical Integration (You Own the Stack)**
- **Before:** You sold cameras; customers had to hire data scientists
- **After:** You provide end-to-end solution; customers just flip a switch
- **Advantage:** Insulation from competitors; stickier customer relationships

---

### 2. **Data Moat (Your Secret Advantage)**
- ML models trained on 7+ years of *your* customer data
- Competitors can't replicate without decades of data
- Your models improve faster as more customers use platform
- **Advantage:** Sustained competitive advantage you can't be out-engineered on

---

### 3. **Predictable Recurring Revenue**
- **Before:** $100K sales every 12-18 months (unpredictable)
- **After:** $2-5K/month per customer (predictable, grows with customer success)
- **Advantage:** Higher valuation multiples; easier to raise capital; easier planning

---

### 4. **Network Effects**
- More customers → more data → better models → more attractive to new customers
- Competitors trapped in chicken-and-egg (need customers to get data, need data to win customers)
- **Advantage:** Winner-take-most market dynamics work in your favor

---

### 5. **Customer Lock-In (Good Kind)**
- Customers' workflows depend on your dashboards and alerts
- Switching costs = retraining, workflow disruption, risk
- **Advantage:** Lower churn; easier upsells; defensive against price competition

---

### 6. **Scalable Operations**
- Add customer #47 without rebuilding infrastructure
- Code reused across all customers (platform economics)
- Self-service analytics (reduced support cost)
- **Advantage:** Unit economics improve with scale; path to profitability

---

### 7. **Adjacent Market Opportunities**
Once you own the customer relationship + have their data, you can:
- Sell **preventive maintenance predictions** (device health)
- Sell **supply chain quality tracking** (across multiple manufacturers)
- License **specialized models** (for specific materials/geometries)
- **Advantage:** Multiple revenue streams from single data asset

## PRICING & GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY

### Tiered Subscription Model

**TIER 1: "Inspect" ($1,500/month)**
- Real-time scan analysis dashboard
- Historical trending (3-month rolling)
- Basic alerts (high-priority defects only)
- Max 10 scans/day
- Typical: Small manufacturers, proof-of-concept customers

**TIER 2: "Optimize" ($5,000/month)**
- Everything in Tier 1, plus:
- 1-year historical data retention
- Advanced analytics (yield analysis, operator KPIs)
- Custom alerts and rules
- Max 100 scans/day
- Typical: Mid-market manufacturers

**TIER 3: "Predict" ($15,000+/month)**
- Everything in Tier 2, plus:
- Full data retention (7 years)
- Custom ML models trained on your data
- Predictive quality (defect prediction before scan completes)
- Device integration (automated alerts to edge)
- Unlimited scans
- Typical: Enterprise customers, high-volume manufacturing

**TIER 4: "Managed Service" (Custom pricing)**
- We host and manage your entire quality operation
- Full automation (minimal human touch)
- Includes implementation, training, ongoing optimization
- 12-month minimum contract
- Typical: Premium customers, strategic partnerships

## RISKS TO THIS STRATEGY

### Technical Risks
- **Model accuracy insufficient:** Mitigated by starting with simple rules, progressing to ML
- **Scale challenges:** Mitigated by cloud architecture (auto-scales)
- **Integration complexity:** Mitigated by Unity Catalog (handles isolation)

### Market Risks
- **Slower adoption than projected:** Mitigated by freemium/pilot programs
- **Customers won't pay for SaaS:** Mitigated by showing ROI (labor savings pay for subscription 5x over)
- **Competitor enters market quickly:** Mitigated by data moat (hard to replicate)

### Organizational Risks
- **Shift from product to service business:** Requires new capabilities (support, operations, customer success)
- **Cannibalization of device sales:** Actually doesn't happen—more often upsells existing customers
- **Team scaling:** Good news—talent is easier to hire for cloud/AI than optical engineering
